Artists

    • Lata Mangeshkar

    Lata Mangeshkar (Name in Hindi:
    लता मंगेशकर; born 28th September
    1929) is an Indian singer.
    Lata Mangeshkar is also called
    the nightingale of India.

    • Asha Bhosle

    Asha Bhosle (Name in Hindi
    : आशा भोसले) (born September
    8, 1933) is an Indian singer.
    She is one of the best-known
    and highly regarded Hindi singer

    • Kishore Kumar

    Kishore Kumar (4 August 1929
    – 13 October 1987) was a popular
    Indian film playback singer, actor,
    lyricist, composer, producer and director.

    • Mohammad Rafi

    Mohammed Rafi (Hindi: मोहम्मद रफ़ी;
    24 December 1924 – 31 July 1980),
    was an Indian playback singer whose
    career spanned four decades.

    • A R Rahman

    Allah Rakha Rahman born 6 January
    1966 in Chennai, India as A. S. Dileep Kumar)
    is an Indian film composer, record producer,
    musician, singer and philanthropist.

    • Sonu Nigam

    Sonu Nigam was born on the 30th
    of July, 1973 in Faridabad, Haryana.
    His father Agam Kumar's passion for music
    was soon imbibed by Sonu in his early
    years and he soon began training under him.

    • Shreya Ghoshal

    Shreya Ghoshal (श्रेया घोषाल) is
    a 2-time National Film Award
    and 6-time Filmfare Award-winning
    Indian playback singer of Bengali origin.

    • Alka Yagnik

    Alka Yagnik (अलका याज्ञिक) is an Indian singer
    who is ranked among the best-known Hindi playback
    singers of all time. She is a seven-time winner of
    the Filmfare Best Female Playback Award and has done
    playback singing for over 700 Indian films.
